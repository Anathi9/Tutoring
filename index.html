About Us

Kusasa Tutoring was founded with the goal of empowering students to achieve academic success. Our founder, Sipho Ngubentombi, started Kusasa Tutoring in the informal settlement of Khayelitsha with a mission to help students improve their academic results. We believe in making a difference in the lives of learners, particularly those struggling to meet their academic expectations. Our services not only focus on helping students achieve their goals but also provide support for those who wish to prepare for supplementary exams. Additionally, for as little as R500, we offer online classes for Pure Mathematics, Physics, Mathematics Literacy, and Life Sciences. With personalized and dedicated support, we aim to bridge the academic gap and unlock the potential of every student.
